# Installation Precaution

Robot installers must install and operate the robots in accordance with the guidelines of ISO 12100 and ISO 10218-2, and installers must comply with the relevant requirements of international standards such as ISO / TS 15066 and national laws.<br>
The manufacturer is not responsible for any accidents caused by risks that do not comply with the relevant requirements provided by international standards, risks that do not comply with the relevant requirements provided by national laws and regulations, or those caused by failure to review the risk assessment in the previous chapter.

Our robots must be installed in compliance with the inspection standards outlined in the "Industrial Safety and Health Regulations" and the "Safety Inspection Notice." For more details, please refer to the content in Appendix M.
