# Safety Controller

The Safety Control System for the Rainbow Robotics robot models listed below meets ISO 13849-1 Cat3. PLd standards:

- RB5-850E Series
- RB3-1200E Series
- RB10-1300E Series
- RB16-900E Series
- RB3-730ES Series
- RB6-920ES Series
- RB20-1900ES Series
